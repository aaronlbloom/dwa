ISBN-10 / ISBN-13 Conversion Tool

Enter an ISBN-10 or ISBN-13* below:

 

As you may be aware, the book industry is changing from 10 to 13 digit ISBNs in January 2007. This tool is intended to provide a simple way of converting between these numbers, if you happen to find it necessary to do so.

* Please note that only ISBN-13 beginning with ‘978’ can be converted to ISBN-10. ISBNs issued from January 2007, with different preceding digits, will not be convertible as the resulting ISBN-10 would not be a unique identifier.
